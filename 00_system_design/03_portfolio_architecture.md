## Planned Scope

This module will cover:

1. **Track Design** — How to design research tracks that are independent but complementary
2. **Exploration vs. Exploitation** — How to balance building on what works vs. testing new directions
3. **Resource Allocation** — How to allocate student and internal team effort across tracks
4. **Track Lifecycle** — Decision criteria for pausing, closing, or splitting a track
5. **Radar Integration** — How the Portfolio connects to the Technology Radar
