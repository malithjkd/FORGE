# Module 3: Collaboration Protocol

> **Document Status:** Placeholder — Not Yet Started  
> **Purpose:** Formalising the PBA–University of Moratuwa working interface  
> **Prerequisite:** Module 1 (Knowledge Architecture) ✅ Complete

---

## Planned Scope

This module will cover:

1. **Contribution Interface** — What format does university work arrive in? How is it integrated into FORGE?
2. **IP & Data Sharing** — What data can students take? What belongs to PBA? What is jointly owned?
3. **Publication Protocol** — How to handle academic publication of jointly-produced work
4. **Escalation Paths** — What happens when a student is stuck, or a supervisor disagrees with PBA's direction?
5. **Alignment Without Micromanagement** — How to maintain strategic direction while preserving academic freedom

## Key Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role | Contribution |
|-------------|------|--------------|
| PBA Research Lead (MJ) | Project sponsor & coordinator | Problem definition, resources, direction |
| UoM Academic Panel | Supervision & academic quality | Methodology guidance, thesis oversight |
| MSc Student 1 | Researcher (Data Collection track) | Experiments, reports, data |
| MSc Student 2 | Researcher (ML Diagnosis track) | Experiments, reports, models |

## Key Questions to Address

- Who owns the trained ML models?
- Can students publish results before PBA has a product?
- What is the minimum reporting cadence between PBA and UoM?
- How do we handle disagreements about methodology?
- What happens to FORGE contributions after a student graduates?
